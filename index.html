TravelR Account Deletion

You can delete your account using one of the following methods:

1. Delete Through the App

  • Open the TravelR app
  • Go to your Profile page
  • Tap the "Delete Account" button
  • Confirm by selecting "Yes, Delete" when prompted

2. Request Deletion by Email

To request account deletion, send an email to aliaktasofficial@gmail.com

Please include your registered email address in your request.

Data Retention Policy

When your account is deleted, all your personal data and content will be permanently removed from our systems.
